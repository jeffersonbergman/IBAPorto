Who We Are

The Antioquia Church is a local church that recognizes and affirms the Lordship of Jesus Christ.

As such, we are committed to striving diligently for the faith that was once for all delivered to the saints from Him (Jude 3).

The essence of this faith is based on the Gospel proclaimed by the Apostles, as well as the underlying instruction, which the Lord commanded to be taught to all who believe (Matthew 28:19-20).
